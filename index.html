OLS Operations Hub — Overview
🚀
Welcome to OLS Operations Hub
Your central command for projects, tenders, and weekly reporting. Built for the One Logistics Solutions team.
1 Dashboard
The main overview page. Shows:
— Active Clients — how many clients have running workstreams
— Tenders In Flight — open RFQ / RFX count
— Due This Week — deadlines in the next 7 days
— Blocked Items — workstreams needing attention
— Portfolio Health donut — live status breakdown
— Tender Pipeline — tender stages at a glance
— This Week's Focus — urgent items listed below
2 Active Projects
Tracks all client workstreams. Each client groups its workstreams together.

To add a project: Click + New Entry → fill in Client Name, Workstream, Service Line, Owner, Status, and This Week plan → Save.

Important: Client name must be spelled exactly the same for grouping to work. Example: always use Mofmofriends not mofmofriends.

To update status: Click any row → change status directly in the detail panel.
3 Tenders
Tracks all RFQ, RFX and direct negotiations.

To add a tender: Click + New Tender → fill in Client, Type, Owner, Status, Document link → add milestones with dates (e.g. Aanwijzing, Submission, Negotiation) → Save.

Statuses: Upcoming → Submitted → Negotiating → Won / Lost
4 Weekly Report
Generates your Monday meeting document automatically.

How it works:
— Last Week is auto-filled from what you entered as This Week previously
— Fill in This Week updates for each workstream
— Click Preview to review before exporting
— Click Export .docx to download — open in Word or Google Docs

Do this every week before your Monday meeting. The system remembers last week automatically.
5 For New Team Members
When opening the Hub on a new device or browser for the first time:

1. Open the Hub URL in Chrome
2. Make sure you are logged in to your @onelogisticssolutions.com Google account
3. Go to Settings → Google Sheets in the sidebar
4. Paste the Web App URL (get this from your team lead)
5. Click Save & Connect
6. You will see ● Connected to Sheets at the bottom of the sidebar

All data is stored in Google Sheets — you are always seeing the latest version.
6 Connected Tools
— Legal Tracker — manages all contracts and legal documents. Opens in a new tab.
— Google Sheets — all Hub data syncs here automatically on every save.
— Export CSV — downloads all projects and tenders as a spreadsheet file.
Need help? Contact your system administrator or reach out via the OLS team WhatsApp group.
